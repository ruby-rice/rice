Detailed Description

A wrapper for ruby's Symbol class.

Symbols are internal identifiers in ruby. They are singletons and can be thought of as frozen strings. They differ from an Identifier in that they are in fact real Objects, but they can be converted back and forth between Identifier and Symbol.
